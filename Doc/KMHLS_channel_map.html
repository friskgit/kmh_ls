Channel mapping plugin for Klangkupolen 45.4

Channel mapping plugin that takes 45+3 inputs and outputs 29 channels linearly, then adds the extra thre channels and appends the last 16 channels for a total of 48 channels. This utility is due to the way outputs are mapped in the standard configuration of the Nexus mixer.

Insert this plugin after the decoder to get channels to map correctly to the Nexus, i.e.:

  • Channel 1-29 of the decoder maps to Nexus 1-29 (Layer A)
  • Channel 30-45 of the decoder maps to Nexus 33-48 (Layer B)
